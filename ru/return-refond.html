FAQ

CHECK OUR FAQS BELOW – THEY WILL ANSWER MOST OF YOUR QUESTIONS

If you didn't find the answer in the FAQs, please feel free to drop an email to our helpful customer service team. We'll get back to you as quickly as possible.

  • What are the main benefits of the Persia Travel Money?

    Save your time and money

    Feel secure

    Experience Iran like a local

    Persia Travel Money card gives you access to the large network of shops and merchants in Iran. By using Persia Travel Money card you can purchase anything in Iran in Iranian Currency, you can also withdraw cash from ATM. Online shopping is another option for our customers; you can use your card to buy souvenirs or anything you need during your stay. By using Persia Travel Money card you don't have to carry cash around or worry about currency exchange while you're out sightseeing.

  • Where can I use my Persia Travel Money card?

    Shopping centers, Tourist Attractions, Restaurants, Hotels, Museums, Entertainment Centers, Parks, Online Shopping and anyplace you need money.

  • Is there any benefit in applying for Persia Travel Money now?

    If you apply the Persia Travel Money online and through this site then you get the option to have access to your card as soon as you arrive in Iran. Then you can start using your Persia Travel Money from the first moment of your journey.

  • How can I order the Persia Travel Money?

    Apply for Persia Travel Money card in the dedicated portal at www.Persiatravelmoney.com. The bank deliberates the application and after confirmation, the card will be delivered to the tourist in the location of their choice either at the airport or other arriving borders.

  • How do I activate my Persia Travel Money card?

    The process to activate your card is easy. You can follow the instructions on the letter attached to your card.

  • How can I load my Persia Travel Money card?

    You can charge your Persia Travel Money card via our website and it will be ready to be spent in Iranian currency. Besides if you carry cash you can change it in selected bank branches and charge your Persia Travel Money.

  • Can I apply for Persia Travel Money card when I am in Iran?

    Yes. You can apply for Persia Travel Money card before starting the journey or during the trip in Iran.
