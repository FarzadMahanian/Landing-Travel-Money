  • What are the main benefits of the TravelMoney®?

    Save your time and money

    Fell secure

    Experience like a local

    Discover more of Iran for less money

    Travel money gives you the access to the large network of shops and merchants in Iran. By using TravelMoney® you can purchase anything in Iran with Iranian Currency, also you can withdraw cash from ATM. In addition online shopping is another option for our customers; you can use your card for buying souvenirs or other items. By using TravelMoney® you don't have to carry cash around or worry about currency exchange while you're out sightseeing. The TravelMoney® also entitles you to a range of great discounts at tourist attractions, restaurants, shops and entertainment options around the country.

  • Where can I use my TravelMoney® card?

    Shopping centers, Tourism attractions, Restaurants, Hotels, Tehran underground, Tehran buses, Museums, entertainment centers, Parks, online shopping and anyplace you need money.

  • Is there any benefit in buying the TravelMoney® now?

    If you purchase the TravelMoney® online and through this site then you get the option to have your order shipped to you before you travel. Then you can start using your TravelMoney® as soon as you arrive in Iran.

  • How can I order the TravelMoney®?

    The tourist either can refer to the nominated banks as soon as their arrival in the airport or before starting the journey, Apply for TravelMoney® card in the dedicated portal (www.TravelMoney®.my). This registration can also be done through the travel agencies in the country of origin. The bank exchange the foreign currency cash to the Iranian Rials and settles the amount in a Debit Card which is reliable and easy for daily transactions purposes. The nominated banks are available all over the cities and provinces to provide TravelMoney® services to the card holders. At the end of the journey, the traveler can withdraw his/ her money from the issued card in the airport and exchange it to desired currency.

  • Can I buy the TravelMoney® when I am in Iran?

    You can register for TravelMoney® at Iranian airports or bank branches in different cities.

  • How do I activate my TravelMoney® card?

    The process to activate your card is easy. You can follow the instructions on the letter attached to your card.
