Why Persia Travel Money

Iran has always been one of the top destinations for tourists from all around the world who aim to visit historical, cultural and religious sites. In order to provide special payment facilities to the tourists who are willing to travel to Iran, we have established a new generation of card and payment solution. We offer and implement a national tourism payment solution to provide facilities and comfort to the international tourists while on a journey in Iran.

Obtain Persia Travel Money

Every tourist can apply for Persia Travel Money card before starting the journey or during the trip in Iran. The card will be delivered to the tourist in the location of their choice either at the airport or other arriving borders. Whenever you have access to your Persia Travel Money card you can use it in Iranian bank and payment networks.

You can charge your Persia Travel Money card via our website and it will be ready to be spent in Iranian currency. Besides if you carry cash you can change it in selected bank branches and charge your Persia Travel Money. The bank exchange the foreign currency cash to the Iranian Rial and settles the amount in a Persia Travel Money card which is reliable and easy to used for daily transactions and everyday purchases. At the end of the journey, you can withdraw your money from the issued card in the airport or ask our customer service team to transfer and exchange it to desired currency.
